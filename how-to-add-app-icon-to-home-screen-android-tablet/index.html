To add an app icon to your home screen on an Android tablet, follow these simple steps:

1. Start by unlocking your Android tablet and navigating to the app drawer, which is usually represented by an icon with a grid of dots or squares.

2. Once you are in the app drawer, find the app you want to add to your home screen. Press and hold the app icon until a menu appears.

3. From the menu, select "Add to Home Screen" or "Add Shortcut". This option may vary depending on your Android tablet's manufacturer or operating system version.

4. After selecting the "Add to Home Screen" option, you will be taken back to your home screen. You can now drag the app icon to your desired location on the home screen.

5. Once you have placed the app icon in your desired location, release your finger to drop the app icon onto the home screen.

6. Congratulations! You have successfully added an app icon to your home screen on your Android tablet. You can now easily access the app by tapping on its icon directly from the home screen.
